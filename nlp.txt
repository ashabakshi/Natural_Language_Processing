What is Nlp?
-NLP (Natural Language Processing) is a field of AI (Artificial Intelligence) that helps computers understand,
     interpret, and generate human language — like English, Hindi, etc.

In simple words:
👉 It’s how machines “read, listen, and talk” like humans.

Real World Applications of NLP:

            Chatbots: automated user conversation
            Translation: language convert (EN ↔ HI)
            Sentiment Analysis: detect emotion in text
            Voice Assistants: speech understanding
            Spam Detection: filter unwanted emails
            Autocomplete: predict next words
            Search Engines: understand queries
            Text Summarization: short from long text
            Resume Screening: filter candidates
            Text Generation: AI writing (answers/code)


**Approaches of NLP**

* Rule-Based Approach:
  Uses predefined grammar rules, patterns, and dictionaries to process text
  Limitation: rigid, cannot handle new words, slang, or complex sentences

* Statistical Approach:
  Uses probability and frequency of words from large datasets to predict meaning
  Limitation: requires large data, ignores deeper context sometimes

* Machine Learning Approach:
  Uses algorithms that learn patterns from labeled data (e.g., classification tasks)
  Limitation: performance depends on training data quality and feature selection

* Deep Learning Approach:
  Uses neural networks (like RNN, LSTM) to understand context, sequence, and meaning
  Limitation: needs huge data, high computation, and time

* Hybrid Approach:
  Combines rule-based + ML/DL to improve accuracy and handle edge cases
  Limitation: complex system design and harder to maintain
